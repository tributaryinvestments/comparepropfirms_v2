Top One Futures Overview

🇺🇸 United States Top One Futures, LLC Cheyenne, WY

Top One Futures (TOF) is a US-based futures prop firm run by Top One Futures, LLC out of Cheyenne, Wyoming. It reports over $25M in trader payouts, 184,000+ accounts opened across 122 countries, and an "Excellent" 4.8/5 Trustpilot rating from 3,300+ reviews. What sets it apart is breadth: six account types spanning both evaluation and instant-funding models — three you pass (Elite Challenge, Elite Access, Elite Daily) and three you buy funded (Instant Sim Funded, S2F Sim PRO, Ignite) — all on a flat 90/10 profit split with payouts processed through Rise in under 4 hours on average.

TOF is best for traders who want a single firm that can match almost any style. Instant Sim Funded is the most popular plan — funded from day one, EOD trailing drawdown, news trading allowed. Elite Challenge is the cheapest entry at $41/mo for a 25K with code MAY, trading a $149 activation fee for a friendly 25% funded consistency rule. Ignite is the budget instant door with a soft-pause daily loss limit, while S2F Sim PRO serves professionals comfortable with intraday drawdown and the strictest ESS gate. Across the board: manual trading only (no bots), no overnight holds, and a tiered 6% → 5% → 4% payout target on most plans.

Expert Review
In-depth review by Jered Klima, Editor in Chief, based on extensive hands-on testing.
Six account types on one 90/10 split, payouts processed in under four hours, and a 4.8 Trustpilot — Top One Futures gives traders more ways in than almost any firm we’ve reviewed in 2026.
— Jered Klima, Editor in Chief

Top One Futures launched in April 2025 and has scaled fast — 184,000+ accounts across 122 countries, more than $25M in trader payouts, and an “Excellent” 4.8/5 Trustpilot from over 3,300 reviews. The pitch is breadth and speed: six account programs on one flat 90/10 split, with payouts processed through Rise in under four hours on average and 24/7 support that the firm says replies in under a minute.

Six programs, three philosophies

The lineup splits cleanly. Three plans make you pass an evaluation: Elite Challenge (the cheap monthly entry, from $41/mo with code MAY, with a $149 activation on pass and 25% funded consistency), Elite Access (one-time fee, no daily loss limit in the eval, on-demand funded payouts behind a 40% consistency rule), and Elite Daily (one phase, no activation, then daily payouts with no funded consistency rule). The other three are instant funding: Instant Sim Funded (EOD drawdown, 20% consistency), S2F Sim PRO (intraday drawdown and the ESS gate — the pro’s plan), and Ignite (the budget door, with a soft-pause daily loss limit and the firm’s tightest 15% consistency rule).

What stands out

The 90/10 split is among the best in the industry and it applies to every account, not just a premium tier. EOD trailing drawdown on most plans is forgiving, payout processing is genuinely fast, and the consistency rules are clearly published per plan rather than buried. Ignite’s soft-pause daily loss limit — a bad day pauses the account until 6pm ET instead of breaching it — is a thoughtful touch most firms don’t offer.

Where to be careful

Top One Futures requires manual trading — bots, EAs, and HFT are banned firm-wide, which is stricter than several competitors. The consistency rules range from a gentle 25% (Challenge) to a demanding 15% (Ignite) and are the most common reason first payouts get held. S2F PRO’s intraday drawdown punishes open-trade wicks in a way EOD traders won’t expect. And the travel rule has real teeth: trading while physically located in a restricted country is a hard breach, not a warning. The homepage also still shows a couple of legacy figures (a $1.2M calculator cap, an older payout example) that lag the current help-center rules — always confirm specifics in the help center before buying.

But for a trader who wants a fair menu of funding paths, a class-leading split, and money that actually arrives quickly, Top One Futures is one of the most versatile firms we’ve reviewed in 2026. Match the program to how you actually trade — budget vs. instant, EOD vs. intraday, daily vs. on-demand — and the rest of the experience holds up.

General FAQ
The most common questions traders ask about Top One Futures, across all six account types.
How fast does Top One Futures actually pay out?

Top One Futures advertises average payout processing of under 4 hours and processes all payouts through Rise (Riseworks.io). Elite Daily funded accounts can request a payout every 24 hours from day one; Elite Access is on-demand once you clear the buffer and profit requirements; and the instant plans (Instant, S2F PRO, Ignite) pay per cycle.
