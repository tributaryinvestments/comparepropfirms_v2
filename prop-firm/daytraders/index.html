DayTraders.com Overview
Everything you need to know at a glance.
🇺🇸 United States Founder: Leo Riot Founded 2023

DayTraders.com is a futures prop firm founded in 2023 and based in Las Vegas, Nevada. It runs two products — Evaluations, offered with three drawdown styles (Trail, EOD, and Static), and Instant Funding (Straight to Funded and Straight to Live). The firm is known for letting traders keep 100% of their profits per account, automated payouts processed through Plane (often in about 30 minutes), and funding up to $1.5M.

DayTraders.com is best for traders who want choice in how their drawdown works. Trail is the cheapest entry with an intraday trailing drawdown; EOD adds an end-of-day buffer; Static gives a simple fixed limit. Straight to Funded skips the evaluation entirely, and Straight to Live puts you on a real live brokerage account after a short 8-day qualifier.

The 30-second verdict

For the cheapest path to funded, start with a Static or Trail 25K (from $22.50–$24.90 with the current 90%-off promo, code WODWQBSZ). Want an end-of-day buffer? Choose EOD. Prefer to skip the evaluation? Go Straight to Funded. Want real live capital with daily payouts? Go Straight to Live. Everything is futures-only, and you keep 100% of your profits per account (Straight to Live is 80/20 on live capital).

Expert Review
In-depth review by Jered Klima, Editor in Chief, based on 18 months of testing.
If you want the fastest, most reliable path to a real funded futures account in 2026, DayTraders.com is the firm to beat — full stop.
— Jered Klima, Editor in Chief

DayTraders.com is the rare prop firm that lives up to its marketing. We tested across 18 months — all three evaluation drawdown styles (Trail, EOD, and Static) plus both instant-funding paths (Straight to Funded and Straight to Live). Across all of them, payouts arrived automatically through Plane, usually in about 30 minutes. That's not normal. Most firms promise fast payouts and deliver slow ones; DayTraders.com reverses the pattern.

Trail & Static: where most traders should start

The Trail evaluation is the cheapest entry, using an intraday trailing drawdown; Static swaps that for a simple fixed limit that never moves. Both are 1-step and can be passed in as little as two qualifying days, with a 50% consistency target during the evaluation. For traders new to prop firms, Static in particular is a gentle on-ramp because the loss line never trails against you.

EOD: the buffer that changes how you trade

For my own trading I reach for EOD. It swaps the intraday trail for an end-of-day trailing drawdown, so a midday spike doesn't take you out — the loss line only trails on closed daily profit. That extra room changes how you can manage a position through news and volatile sessions while still keeping a defined floor underneath you.

Straight to Funded & Straight to Live

For traders ready to skip evaluation, Straight to Funded pays once and puts you on a funded account immediately, with a tight 20% consistency rule. Straight to Live goes further: after a short 8-day qualifier you trade a real live brokerage account with daily payouts on an 80/20 split. The catch across instant funding is that there are no resets — a breached account is gone, and you buy again (usually at a discount).

Where DayTraders.com isn't perfect

The 20% consistency rule on Straight to Funded is the main friction point — one big day caps how much the rest can contribute. There are no resets, so a breach ends the account. And evaluations carry a $130 activation fee on top of the one-time price (instant-funding accounts are $0).

But for the typical futures trader — pass once, get funded, and collect automated payouts — DayTraders.com is one of the most direct routes there is. Its combination of competitive pricing, a true 100% profit split (Pro & S2F), and automated payouts that land in about half an hour is hard to beat.

General FAQ
The most common questions traders ask about DayTraders.com, across all account types.
How fast does DayTraders.com actually pay out?

Payouts are fully automated through Plane and typically arrive in about 30 minutes — DayTraders.com's published average is around 32 minutes. The minimum payout is $500, in $500 increments. Funded Pro accounts can request after 8 qualifying days, Straight to Funded after 10, and Straight to Live pays daily once your trailing balance has locked.

What's the difference between Trail, EOD, Static, S2F, and S2L?

Trail, EOD, and Static are the three Evaluation drawdown styles — intraday trailing, end-of-day trailing, and a fixed limit that never moves — all 1-step with a 50% consistency target. Straight to Funded (S2F) skips the evaluation and funds you instantly with a 20% consistency rule. Straight to Live (S2L) puts you on a real live brokerage account after an 8-day qualifier, paid daily on an 80/20 split.

What's the consistency rule and how does it work?

It limits how much of your total profit can come from a single trading day when you request a payout. During evaluations the target is 50%. Once funded, it tightens to 30% on Funded Pro, 20% on Straight to Funded, and 25% on Straight to Live. A single oversized day doesn't breach the account — it just delays a payout until you've built more balanced profit.

What platforms does DayTraders.com support?

The proprietary Onyx platform (with TradingView charting built in) plus Rithmic-connected platforms including Quantower, Sierra Chart, MotiveWave, and Jigsaw. Free real-time market data is included on all accounts, and Onyx offers mobile access so you can manage positions on the go.

Can I trade news events on DayTraders.com?

Yes — DayTraders.com allows news trading, scalping, and bot/automated trading (with ownership verification). Martingale and dollar-cost-averaging are allowed. Hedging across accounts is not permitted.

Can I hold positions overnight or over the weekend?

No. All positions must be flat by 4:59 PM ET each trading day; overnight and weekend holding isn't permitted on any account, regardless of type.

How many funded accounts can I have?

Up to 15 on Trail and Static, and up to 5 on EOD, Straight to Funded, and Straight to Live. Total funding scales up to $1.5M. Each account is treated independently for drawdown, daily loss limits, and consistency calculations.

What happens if I breach my account?

There are no resets. A breached account — evaluation or funded — is closed, and you'd purchase a new one to try again. DayTraders.com frequently offers a discount on a fresh attempt rather than a traditional reset.

Is DayTraders.com legit?

Yes. DayTraders.com is a US-based futures prop firm founded in 2023 and based in Las Vegas, Nevada. It runs automated payouts through Plane, funds traders up to $1.5M, and holds a 4.6/5 Trustpilot rating from 440+ verified reviews.
