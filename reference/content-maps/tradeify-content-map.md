## Company
- **Tradeify** — futures prop firm. Launched **June 2024** by **Brett Simberkoff**. HQ **Boca Raton, FL, USA**.
- **$125M+** paid to traders; **60,000+** funded traders.
- **Trustpilot 4.6/5** from **2,549+** reviews.
- **CPF editorial score (this page): 5.0/5.**
- Positioning: fastest, most reliable path to a funded futures account; payouts within 24–72h of approval.
- "Funded" = **Simulated Funded Account** (trades mirror live markets; P&L not real capital). Live capital comes via the **Elite Live** program.

## Payout policy by type
- **Growth (5-winning-day cycle):** min payout 25K $250 / 50K $500 / 100K $1,000 / 150K $1,500; per-request cap 25K $1,000 / 50K $3,000 / 100K $4,000 / 150K $5,000; 35% funded consistency. Min balance to request (50K) = $53,000.
- **Select Flex:** payout after **5 winning days** (frequency reads "**5 winning days**"); up to **50% of total profit**, capped 25K $1,250 / 50K $3,000 / 100K $4,000 / 150K $5,000; **no funded consistency**, no DLL.
- **Select Daily:** request after one qualifying day; up to **2× profit since last payout**, capped 25K $600 / 50K $1,000 / 100K $1,500 / 150K $2,500; buffer = start + drawdown + $100; min payout $250.
- **Lightning (goal-based, progressive consistency 20% → 25% → 30%):** goal1/goal2 per size (25K $1,500/$1,000 fixed-$1,000 payout · 50K $3,000/$2,000 · 100K $6,000/$3,500 · 150K $9,000/$4,500); **no resets** (breach = dead account).
- Processing: 24–48h after approval (within 24h on Lightning); up to 72h outside business hours.

## Evaluation rules
- **Growth:** 1-step; pass in as little as **1 day**; **no consistency rule** during eval.
- **Select:** 1-step; **min 3 trading days**; **40%** consistency during eval only; **no DLL** during eval.
- **Lightning:** no evaluation.

---

## Trading rules
- **Allowed:** news trading, bots/algos (ownership verification), copy trading (Tradovate copier), Martingale/DCA, VPN/VPS.
- **Prohibited:** hedging, HFT, tick scalping, latency arbitrage, reverse arbitrage, market-data abuse.
- **No overnight/weekend holding** — flat before the daily close (4:59 PM ET). Weekly activity required on funded accounts.

## Restricted countries (two tiers — verify at help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10495888)
- **Sim Funded Only** (27): can trade evals + earn payouts at 90/10, but **not** Live Funded. Includes Canada, China, Hong Kong, South Korea, UAE, South Africa, Philippines, etc.
- **Not Available** (58): residents not eligible (sanctions/compliance). Eligibility by **residency**, not citizenship; travel is OK; **no VPN bypass**; KYC required.
