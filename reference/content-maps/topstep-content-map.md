## Three-stage journey
1. **Trading Combine®** — monthly-subscription evaluation.
2. **Express Funded Account® (XFA)** — simulated funded; earns real payouts.
3. **Live Funded Account® (LFA)** — real capital, invite-only (~**0.71%** of XFAs reached it in 2025).

### Trading Combine
- **One hard rule:** don't hit the Maximum Loss Limit (MLL).
- **Objectives to pass:** profit target + consistency (best single day **< 50%** of the profit target).
- No minimum trading days, no time limit.

### Express Funded Account (XFA)
- Balance **starts at $0**; grows from profits. Up to **5 active** XFAs.
- At activation, choose **payout mode: Standard or Consistency** (locked per account; mix allowed up to the 5-account limit).
- **90/10 split.** Optional Daily Loss Limit. Scaling Plan. MLL.
- Breach MLL -> account closed. **Back2Funded:** up to 2 paid reactivations before your first payout.

### Live Funded Account (LFA)
- Invite-only; size = average of payout-eligible XFAs (max **$150K**).
- Dynamic Live Risk Expansion; up to **$250K+** performance bonuses.
- 30 winning days -> daily payouts + up to 100% balance access.

## Payout policy *(verified help.topstep.com payout policy, 2026)*
- **90/10 split** from dollar one for accounts created **after Jan 12, 2026** (pre-Jan-12-2026 traders keep 100% of first $10K, then 90/10). Page uses 90/10.
- **Min payout $125.** Requested **on demand** during CME hours (**Sun 5 PM - Fri 5 PM CT**).
- Per-request cap = **50% of balance**, up to the cap below (**XFA only**; LFA uncapped):

| Size | Standard cap | Consistency cap |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| 50K  | $2,000       | $3,000          |
| 100K | $3,000       | $4,000          |
| 150K | $5,000       | $6,000          |

- **Standard mode:** 5 winning days of $150+ net; **no** consistency rule.
- **Consistency mode:** 3 trading days; best single day **<= 40%** of total profit.
- ** The two cap amounts are set by payout MODE (Standard vs Consistency), NOT by the Daily Loss Limit.**
- After each payout: MLL -> $0; day-count resets.
- **Methods:** Aeropay (US, instant, no Topstep fee), Wise (intl + domestic, 1-3 days, no Topstep fee), ACH (US, 1-3 days, **$30**), Wire/SWIFT (intl, 5-10 days, **$30**).
- Tax: W-9 (US) / W-8BEN (non-US). Own-name bank only.

## Trading rules
- **Day-trading only.** Flat by **3:10 PM CT**; session reopens **5:00 PM CT**. No swing/overnight; no spot forex.
- **Allowed:** news trading, automated/algos, **trade copiers** (up to **$750K** buying power across XFAs), DCA/averaging.
- **Prohibited:** cross-account hedging & coordinated trading, spoofing, **VPN/proxy/TOR**, chargebacks, account stacking.
- **Instruments:** CME / CBOT / NYMEX / COMEX futures + micros.
